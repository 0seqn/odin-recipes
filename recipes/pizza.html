pizza

This is the perfect pizza for anybody to eat with perfect delicious cheese

If you want a beautiful chunky pizza this is the best pizza for you!

Ingredients

  • 300g strong bread flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • For the tomato sauce
  • 100ml passata
  • handful fresh basil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • For the topping
  • 125g ball mozzarella
  • handful grated or shaved parmesan
  • handful of cherry tomatoes
  • To finish
  • handful of basil leaves (optional)

Steps

  1. Get your kitchen pieces to create the pizza
  2. create the base for the pizza
  3. add all of your ingredients
  4. put it in the cooker
  5. take it out and make a sauce if you want
  6. Enjoy your delicious pizza
