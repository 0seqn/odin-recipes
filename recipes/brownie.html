Brownie

This is the perfect brownie for anybody to eat!

If you want a rich chocolate filled brownie this is the best for you!

Ingredients

  • Chocolate chunks
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Salt
  • Cocoa
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Get your bowl to create the brownie
  2. Melt the chocolate and stir it in the bowl
  3. Add dry ingredients
  4. Add choc chips if you have them
  5. Let it sit in the fridge for a couple hours
  6. Then take it out, slice it into pieces and you have the most delicious and easiest brownies
